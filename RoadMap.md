# 📝 Learning Workflow

For every topic:

```
Learn the concept
        ↓
Implement it from scratch
        ↓
Solve 10–20 Easy problems
        ↓
Solve 10–20 Medium problems
        ↓
Review mistakes
        ↓
Move to the next topic
```

---

# 📌 Problem-Solving Checklist

For every problem, ask yourself:

- [ ] Do I fully understand the problem?
- [ ] Can I solve it using brute force?
- [ ] Can I optimize it?
- [ ] Do I know the time complexity?
- [ ] Do I know the space complexity?
- [ ] Can I explain my solution to someone else?
- [ ] Have I committed it to GitHub?

---

# 📖 Golden Rules

- ✅ Understand before memorizing.
- ✅ Write the brute-force solution first.
- ✅ Optimize only after the brute-force works.
- ✅ Analyze time and space complexity.
- ✅ Review solved problems regularly.
- ✅ Focus on consistency rather than speed.
- ❌ Never copy solutions without understanding them.

# 🎯 Final Goal

By the end of this roadmap, I aim to:
